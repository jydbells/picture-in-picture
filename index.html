Quick Guide
- picture-in-picture app is made for users who don't have a multi-screen set-up.
- if you select window, click 'START' to begin sharing OR if you select tab, navigate back to picture-in-picture app and click the 'START' to begin sharing.
- the pop-up mini-screen can be resized from its default size as well as repositioned anywhere on your computer screen to suit your need.
- reload the app to choose a new window/tab to share.
